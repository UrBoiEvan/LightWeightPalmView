About Me

I'm Evan Harden, a media artist with a technical edge. As a computer cience student at the University of Florida with a minor in Digital Arts and Sciences, I sit at the intersection of technology and creativity—bringing structure and imagination together to craft compelling real estate media.

What started as a creative outlet has grown into a refined craft: blending cinematic video, precise photography, and intentional editing to elevate real estate listings. As a media artist, I focus on creating immersive visuals with a smooth flow and standout presence.

I'm FAA Part 107 certified for commercial drone work, and Adobe certified in After Effects and Premiere Pro. I handle everything from drone flights to color grades with both precision and purpose.

Whether you're listing a cozy home or a luxury space, I aim to deliver clean, experience-focused media that leaves a lasting impression. Contact me to get started.
